blood Center

Donate blood and save lives.

Donating blood not only saves people's lives, but blood donors are also benefited in many ways, some of them are - regular blood donation reduces the risk of cancer, giving blood three times a year increases the rate of new red blood cell production in the body, keeps the bone marrow active and Blood deficiency is quickly replenished.

Services

Our service is to provide free blood to various patients so that a patient can recover easily

We have around 2000 volunteers working with us who help a patient get free blood by guiding different donors.
